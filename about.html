Workshop Coordinator

Novice2Expert Coding Club

To give a little bit of background about this club: it's a student run club where we offer block based coding workshops to students who come to us with little to knowledge of coding. Our goal essentially is to expose students to coding and increase their knowledge from Novice to Expert levels!

The N2E Coding Club just started off Spring of 2016, and I am one of the lucky people to have been with this club since the very begining. I was initially attracted to this club because I love coding and I wanted to get everyone else as excited as I am about it! I joined the board as the workshop coordinator, and my job is to facilitate the coding workshops by finding instructors to teach them, getting the students to sign up for the right classses, booking rooms where the workshops can be taught, etc.

Aside from being a board memeber, I also teach python with N2E Coding Club. Python was the first coding language I created a project with , so I find it such a great starter language and I hope I can introduce my students to it well enough for them to create projects on their own someday too!

Technology Chair

Bengali Students Association

BSA is a student run organization dedicated to celebrating the Bengali culture. Aside from our Bengali themed general meetings, we have big events every year where we showcase our culture with music, food, performances, and more. Joining BSA in my freshman year was one of the best decisions I made in college! While I was antisocial in the first couple of meetings, the board members invited me in as one of there own and made me feel at home. I was able to meet many people that share the same love for Bangladesh as me and I made many irreplaceable friends. As I became more involved with the club, I was also encouraged to act in play for the Bengali New Years! Although I was very scared at first, with the help of my friends, I was able to break out of my shell and perform in front of hundreds of poeple.

This year I joined the BSA Board and became the Technology Chair. My work involves creating flyers and presentations to promote our general meetings and big events. I also oversee all technical aspects of our events. I really enjoy being a part of the board that puts together such fun activitiesand I hope to try my hardest to make all our events very successful!
